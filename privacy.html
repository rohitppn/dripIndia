Privacy Policy

Last updated: 28 April 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how DripIndia ("we", "our", "us") handles information in connection with our content channels, including the Telegram channel @dripindia_official, our Pinterest profile, our Instagram account, and any related social media accounts (collectively, the "Service"). DripIndia is an independent affiliate publisher curating fashion deals for Indian shoppers.

1. Who We Are

DripIndia is operated by an independent publisher based in India. We do not sell products directly. We curate publicly listed fashion products from retailers including but not limited to Myntra, Ajio, Meesho, Flipkart, Amazon India, and Snapdeal, and share links to those products with our community.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information you give us directly

We do not run a website that asks you to sign up, log in, or submit forms. The only direct information we receive is when you voluntarily contact us by email, in which case we receive your email address and the contents of your message.

2.2 Information collected automatically

We do not place cookies, tracking pixels, or analytics scripts on this website. The only network requests this site makes are to load the page itself.

2.3 Information collected by platforms we publish on

When you interact with our content on Telegram, Pinterest, or Instagram, those platforms collect data about that interaction according to their own privacy policies. We do not control or have access to that platform-collected data beyond aggregate statistics those platforms make available to us as a publisher (for example, follower count, post views, or pin clicks).

2.4 Affiliate links and third-party retailers

Posts and pins published by DripIndia may contain affiliate links. When you click an affiliate link, the destination retailer (and possibly an affiliate network such as EarnKaro or similar) may set cookies or otherwise track that referral so that we can be credited if you make a purchase. Any data collection at that point is governed by the retailer's and affiliate network's own privacy policies, not ours.

3. How We Use Information

  • To respond to email enquiries you send us.
  • To understand which posts and pins resonate with our community (using only aggregate platform statistics).
  • To improve the curation we publish.

We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Pinterest API Usage

DripIndia uses the Pinterest API solely to publish curated fashion deal pins to our own Pinterest profile and boards, and to read aggregate metadata about our own boards and pins (for example, board lists and pin counts) so that we can manage and organise our content programmatically.

  • We do not access or store data about other Pinterest users.
  • We do not use Pinterest API data for advertising, ad-targeting, or resale.
  • We do not share Pinterest API data with third parties.
  • Access tokens we obtain from Pinterest are stored as encrypted environment variables on our infrastructure and used only to authenticate our own publishing requests.

Our use of the Pinterest API is bound by Pinterest's Developer Terms, Privacy Policy and Community Guidelines.

5. Affiliate Disclosure

DripIndia participates in affiliate marketing programs. This means some of the links in our posts and pins are affiliate links: if you click and make a purchase at the destination retailer, we may earn a small commission at no additional cost to you. This is how we sustain the channel. We disclose this on our Telegram channel description, on our Pinterest profile bio, and within individual posts where required by platform rules or applicable law.

6. Children's Privacy

DripIndia is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. If you believe a child has interacted with us, please contact us and we will remove anything received in error.

7. Data Retention

We retain email correspondence for as long as needed to respond and for our records. We do not retain any other personal data because we do not collect any.

8. Your Rights

Because we do not collect personal data through this site, there is generally nothing for us to access, correct, or delete on request. If you have emailed us and would like that correspondence deleted, contact us and we will action your request.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision. Material changes will be announced on our Telegram channel.

10. Contact

For privacy questions, affiliate disclosure questions, takedown requests, or anything else, email us at dripindia.support@gmail.com.
