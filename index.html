What we do on Pinterest

We publish curated pins of fashion products on sale, organized into category boards (Women's Fashion, Men's Fashion, Footwear, Bags & Accessories). Each pin links to the retailer's product page so users can shop directly. We use the Pinterest API to publish our daily curation efficiently and to keep our boards fresh.
